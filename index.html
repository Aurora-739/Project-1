Our Story

Story

We are a small bookshop located just outside of Exeter. The bookshop has been run by family for 3 generations now and have a wonderful mix of old and new books, many of which are dontated by our generous patrons. We aim to create a warm and cozy environment for all those who step foot inside. We thrive off beeing a local business welcoming everyone into our walls. With plenty of comfy seating, a few tables and free wifi anyone can come and browse or just spend as much time as needed surrounded by books.


What makes this bookshop special is its personal touch. We adore talking to our customers about their latest reads and reccomending new ones. The store also hosts local author events, book clubs, and writing workshops, further cultivating a strong sense of community.
